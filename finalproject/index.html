Accra Communities Fighting Illegal Fishing through Awareness

Illegal fishing is a major crisis in Ghana, particularly affecting Accra's coastal communities such as Jamestown and Tema, with the country losing tens of millions of dollars annually due to these practices

"Crime Prevention Cannot work without you. The importance of crime prevention comes from the involvement of the community working together with law enforcement..."

The Accra Illegal Fishing Awareness Center is committed do reducing illegal fishing through education and community involvement. We strive to provide you Accra's most up to date crime data to decrease your chances of becoming one of those victims.
