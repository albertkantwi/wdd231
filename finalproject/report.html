Safety is Everyone's Responsibility

The information on this website is provided as a service to the Denver Community. Remaining vigilant and informed is the best way to deter becoming a victim of crime. The information provided has been gathered by the City and County of Denver and the Denver Police Department. The information is available on the Denver Open data library. It has been extracted and presented in a form that may be easier for some users to interact with and understand. This website is not intended to be your only source of information and you are encouraged to seek out other information to help keep you safe.

Participate in your own well being!
